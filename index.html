About Us

How Can We Help?

We are here for you. We work hard to be a resource for our community. Have ideas about how we could do that better? Join us!

As a Non-Profit organisation we are determined to promote YOGA in community for health and wellbeing of  people's health to combat any unprecedented pandemics like COVID and its impacts that caused widespread panic, stress, and anxiety. Yoga is a known practice that reduces stress and anxiety and enhance immunity. 

Get Involved!

We are always looking for volunteers to help us make our vision a reality. We'll help you find a way to volunteer that best suits you. 

This program is  aimed to...

(1) create awareness that Yoga in daily routine is beneficial for physical and mental health, and 

(2) to promote healthy lifestyle that is instrumental in coping with stress associated with Covid or other personal/ health issues.
